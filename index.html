About The Event

The is an invitation for the Inaugural Ceremony of the Computer Society of India (CSI) - CBIT Student Branch, scheduled for 24th January 2025 at the N-Block Seminar Hall, CBIT. The event features distinguished guests, including Shri C Sudhakar (Chairman, CSI Hyderabad Chapter), Dr. A V Krishna Prasad (Vice Chairman, CSI Hyderabad Chapter), and Dr. D V Ramana (Secretary, CSI Hyderabad Chapter), with sessions including the inaugural ceremony, a tea break, and a technical session.
